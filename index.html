About Smartmod

Built on a Legacy of Craft

Furniture First. Interior Second.

Smartmod is a specialized brand dedicated exclusively to modular kitchens and wardrobes. We were born from a company with over a decade of experience in full home customization and premium furniture solutions.

While our parent brand handles complete interiors, Smartmod focuses its entire expertise, technology, and passion on two things: Kitchens and Wardrobes. This singular focus allows us to deliver a level of quality and innovation that generalist companies simply cannot match.

Every Smartmod product is designed, manufactured, and installed by specialists — not generalists. That is the Smartmod promise.
